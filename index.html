About Us

Green Urban is dedicated to creating a cleaner, greener planet. We tackle sustainability by head-on, collecting and recycling materials, reducing landfill pollution. Our mission is to foster sustainable communities through innovative waste solutions and eco-friendly products.

Services

What We Do.

From waste collection to eco-friendly product sales, we empower communities to protect the environment while benefiting economically.

Collection

Recyclable materials are collected and a fair price is given to the seller, Garbage is collected via price per kg.

Selling

Starch bag, paper bag, cloth bag, Aluminium and degradable straws are sold to the customer which help shaping our environment.
